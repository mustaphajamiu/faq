FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers a variety of free challenges to help you get started. There are also premium challenges for subscribers.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, all projects you complete from Frontend Mentor are yours to use in your portfolio, regardless of whether you have a free or premium subscription.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

You can get help by joining the Frontend Mentor community on Discord, where thousands of developers offer support and feedback on projects.
